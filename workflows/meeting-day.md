# Meeting Day Workflow

**When**: Any day with external meetings
**Duration**: 15 min prep + 10 min post per meeting
**Skills used**: [meeting-prep](../skills/meeting-prep/SKILL.md), [post-meeting-brief](../skills/post-meeting-brief/SKILL.md), [email-composer](../skills/email-composer/SKILL.md)

---

## Before Meetings (Morning)

### 1. Check Today's Calendar
```
What meetings do I have today? For each external meeting,
prepare a brief using the meeting-prep skill.
```

### 2. Review Each Brief
For each meeting, scan the brief for:
- Who am I meeting and what do I know?
- What's the history and current deal stage?
- What's my strategic angle?
- What's my desired outcome?

Priority order if you have multiple meetings:
1. Revenue opportunities (active deals)
2. Strategic partnerships
3. Existing client check-ins
4. Exploratory / networking

---

## During Meetings

Take brief notes focused on:
- **Decisions made** (concrete, not vibes)
- **Commitments** — what YOU promised and what THEY promised
- **Surprises** — anything unexpected that changes the picture
- **Next step** — what happens after this meeting?

You don't need to transcribe everything. Capture the "so what."

---

## After Each Meeting (Within 1 Hour)

### 3. Process Meeting Notes
```
Process my notes from the [Company] meeting.
Use the post-meeting-brief skill.
Here are my notes: [paste meeting notes]
```

### 4. Send Follow-Up
```
Draft a follow-up email based on the post-meeting brief.
Use the email-composer skill.
Include: [key points to reference]
```
→ Review → Approve → Send

### 5. Update CRM
- Update deal stage if it changed
- Create tasks for YOUR action items
- Log notes and next steps
- Schedule follow-up if needed

---

## End of Meeting Day

### 6. Reflect & Log
```
Log today's key meeting takeaways in brain/learnings.md.
Focus on: relationship changes, new intel, commitments.
```

---

## Checklist
After every meeting day:
- [ ] All meetings have post-briefs saved
- [ ] All follow-up emails sent (with approval)
- [ ] CRM updated for every deal discussed
- [ ] Action items have owners and deadlines
- [ ] Key learnings logged to brain
