# Deal Progression Workflow

**When**: After any deal interaction (meeting, email exchange, proposal)
**Duration**: 10-15 minutes
**Skills used**: [post-meeting-brief](../skills/post-meeting-brief/SKILL.md), [email-composer](../skills/email-composer/SKILL.md)

---

## Steps

### 1. Record What Happened
After any deal interaction:
```
Process my notes from the [Company] interaction.
Use the post-meeting-brief skill to extract action items.
```

### 2. Assess Stage Movement
Ask honestly:
- Did the deal actually progress? (Don't inflate stages)
- Is the close date still realistic?
- Did the amount change?
- Are there new stakeholders or blockers?

### Stage Definitions (Customize for your CRM)

| Stage | Criteria to Enter | Typical Duration |
|-------|------------------|-----------------|
| **Lead** | Identified opportunity, no conversation yet | 1-7 days |
| **Discovery** | Had initial conversation, exploring fit | 1-2 weeks |
| **Qualification** | Confirmed budget, need, timeline, authority | 1-2 weeks |
| **Proposal** | Sent formal proposal or SOW | 1-3 weeks |
| **Negotiation** | Active pricing/terms discussion | 1-2 weeks |
| **Closed Won** | Signed agreement, payment terms set | — |
| **Closed Lost** | Explicitly declined or went dark 30+ days | — |

### 3. Update CRM
- Move deal to correct stage
- Update close date and amount
- Add next step with a deadline
- Log key notes from the interaction

### 4. Follow Up
If the interaction warrants a follow-up email:
```
Draft a follow-up email to [contact] referencing our [meeting/call]
today. Use the email-composer skill.
```

### 5. Update Pipeline State
```
Update brain/pipeline.md with the new deal state.
```

---

## Red Flags That Should Trigger Action
- Deal has been in same stage for 2x the typical duration
- Prospect canceled or rescheduled 2+ times
- New competitor mentioned in conversation
- Budget conversation is being avoided
- Key champion left the company
- "Let me check with my team" with no follow-up date
